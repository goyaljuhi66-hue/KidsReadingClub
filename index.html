Welcome to Kids Reading Club

Helping Every Child Read with Confidence

Welcome to Kids Reading Club, a place where children discover the joy of reading and develop the confidence to become independent learners. We specialize in online phonics classes designed to help young readers build strong literacy skills in a fun, engaging, and supportive environment.

We believe that reading is one of the most important skills a child can learn. It opens doors to knowledge, imagination, creativity, and lifelong success. Our goal is to make reading enjoyable and accessible for every child, regardless of their starting point.

Meet Our Expert Teachers

Our teachers are passionate educators who specialize in early childhood literacy and phonics instruction. With a deep understanding of how children learn to read, they create a nurturing, engaging, and supportive learning environment where every child can thrive.

What Makes Our Teachers Special?

    Child-Friendly Approach: Our teachers build strong connections with children, making learning fun, engaging, and stress-free.

    Positive Learning Environment: Every class is designed to boost confidence, celebrate progress, and encourage a love for reading.

    Patient and Polite Educators: Our teachers are kind, respectful, and supportive, ensuring that every child feels heard and encouraged.

    Phonics Specialists: Trained in effective phonics teaching methods that help children develop strong reading and writing skills.

Our Phonics Reading Programs

At Kids reading club, we understand that every child learns at a different pace. That's why our phonics courses are carefully designed according to your child's age, learning level, and reading readiness. Our structured programs help children build strong reading and writing skills step by step.

Rising Reader (Ages 3–4 Years)

A perfect beginner-level phonics program designed to build a strong foundation in reading.

What Your Child Will Learn:

    Letter recognition

    All 26 letter sounds

    Blending skills up to 4-letter words

    15 essential sight words

    Early reading confidence and vocabulary development

Course Duration:

48 Classes

By the end of this course, your child will be able to recognize letters and sounds confidently and start reading simple words independently.

Strong Reader (Ages 4–6 Years)

An advanced phonics program that helps children become confident readers and writers.

What Your Child Will Learn:

    44+ phonics sounds

    Important spelling rules and patterns

    Reading and writing of 8–10 letter words independently

    60 high-frequency sight words

    Improved fluency, comprehension, and spelling skills

Course Duration:

66 Classes

By the end of this course, your child will be able to read and write longer words with confidence and apply phonics rules independently.

Pro Reader (Ages 7+ Years)

A comprehensive program for children who want to master reading and writing skills.

What Your Child Will Learn:

    Complete revision of all phonics sounds

    Advanced spelling rules and word-building strategies

    Reading and writing of 10+ letter words independently

    Enhanced fluency, accuracy, and vocabulary

Course Duration:

30 Classes

By the end of this course, your child will have strong reading and writing abilities, enabling them to tackle complex words and texts with confidence.
